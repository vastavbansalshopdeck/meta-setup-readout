A · Methodology & data cleaning

Source: the live-pilot scheduling sheet, filtered to 7 & 8 July 2026. 9 July was excluded as an in-progress day with sparse logging.

Definitions

  • Served = seller entered a POC breakout room (has a breakout-join time and a named breakout POC).
  • Completed = the sheet's "Was meta setup completed" flag = Yes.
  • Call duration = breakout exit − breakout join.
  • Wait time = breakout join − waiting-room join.
  • Breakout POC is the agent who ran the call — distinct from the "OB POC" (the seller's onboarding owner). All per-agent stats use the breakout POC.
  • Occupancy = total on-call minutes ÷ online span (first join → last exit).
  • Effective POCs = time-weighted headcount = POC-hours available ÷ day length, to correct for staggered staffing.

Time parsing

Timestamps were entered inconsistently (mixed 12/24-hour, dot notation like 4.45, semicolons, missing AM/PM). A parser normalised these using a business-hours window (10 AM–10 PM): bare numbers 1–9 read as PM. Estimated accuracy ±10 min per the logging caveat.

Known data-quality issues
  • Stray AM timestamps: Shrusti (06:15) and Imteyaz (05:00) on 8 July inflate their online spans — real spans ~7h; their occupancy figures are unreliable.
  • Overlapping intervals: Darius on 8 July shows 109% occupancy (two calls logged to one POC simultaneously) — a logging error.
  • Undercounted afternoon arrivals (8 July): many afternoon completed calls have blank waiting-room-join times, so that day's demand curve looks more front-loaded than reality.
  • Non-completions under-logged: short-stay lobby exits at peak may be missing → true completion rate likely slightly below 74%, true abandonment above the ~9 clean cases observed.

F · Failure taxonomy

From remarks on the 33 not-completed calls, blockers are overwhelmingly seller-side and structural:

CategoryTypical remarkFix
Facebook / 2FA / restriction"FB blocked", "2FA code not received", "account restricted", "no eligible FB account"Seller-side account fix + reconnect
Not equipped"laptop not available", "network issue", "couldn't share screen"Desktop required; pre-qualify
Seller left mid-call"order dispatch", "picking up kids", "urgent meeting"Reconnect path

All three are knowable before the call — a pre-qualification screen (desktop + working FB login) would deflect a large share into a "fix this first" flow.

H · Staffing model — assumptions & formulas

Effective POCs (7 Jul) = (4 POC x 2.5h + 10 POC x 6.5h) / 9h = 8.3 Effective POCs (8 Jul) = ~9.5 (churn in/out, ~9-10 sustained) Meta people = Meta target/day ÷ setups per POC/day OB-task people = (OB sellers/day x hrs per seller) ÷ 6.5 productive hrs/person Total = (Meta people + OB people) x 1.15 buffer OB bundle per seller = webd 0.5 + DTR 0.5 + PG 0.25 + fund 0.25 + calls 0.25 = 1.75h Best case (webd & DTR automated) = 1.25h ; Worst = 1.75h Meta setups per POC/day: best 6.0, worst 4.5 (pilot ran 4.8-5.8 effective)
